About the Conference

The Cyprus Academy of Sciences, Letters and Arts in collaboration with the KIOS Research and Innovation Center of Excellence co-organize the "Cyprus AI Conference", on 5-7 November 2025, at the University of Cyprus Campus.

The conference aims to discuss the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence across sectors. From education and ethics to economy, democracy, critical infrastructure, energy and health, this interdisciplinary conference highlights both the opportunities and challenges of AI in the rapidly evolving environment of Cyprus.

The Conference will be held under the auspices of the President of the Republic of Cyprus, H.E. Nicos Christodoulides.

Audience

The conference is addressed to a wide-ranging audience, including but not limited to:

  • Academic and research community
  • Policymakers
  • Legal experts
  • Engineers
  • Representatives of the public and private sector in Cyprus
  • Professionals and business leaders in finance, technology, energy, IT
  • Citizens interested in the impact of artificial intelligence

Conference Format

Daily Structure

Each day will include a maximum of four 90-minute sessions.

Session Format

Each session will consist of four presentations (15 minutes each), followed by a panel discussion with the presenters, moderated by the session chair.

Keynote Talks

There is also the possibility of including keynote talks, potentially on the first day.
