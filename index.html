About the Conference

The Cyprus Academy of Sciences, Letters and Arts in collaboration with the KIOS Research and Innovation Center of Excellence co-organize the "Cyprus AI Conference", on 5-7 November 2025, at the University of Cyprus.

The conference aims to discuss the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence across sectors. From education and ethics to economy, democracy, critical infrastructure, energy and health, this interdisciplinary conference highlights both the opportunities and challenges of AI in the rapidly evolving environment of Cyprus.
